Our Model Built On Service, Transparency & Partnership About STP Investment Services STP was formed in 2008, with one client, five employees and $2B in assets under administration. Today, we’re an award-winning, industry-leading fintech and global investment operations firm that provides complete front, middle, and back office services. The times may change, but not our commitment to living up to the highest standards of service excellence. Service. Transparency. Partnership. That’s STP. History 2020 STP crosses $250B in assets serviced. Global team expands to 220 strong. 2020 2020 STP beats out several larger competitors to win FTF’s “Best Outsourcing Provider” award after votes from industry colleagues and partners. 2020 2020 STP acquires Accusource to expand the breadth of services to RIAs, family offices, and asset managers. Read the Story 2019 2019 STP acquires AlphaOne Capital to expand trading, compliance, and emerging manager services. 2019 STP hosts its first-ever client conference, a two-day event in Philadelphia, PA. Clients from across the country participate. 2017 STP team grows to 100 strong across our offices in West Chester, PA and Bangalore, India. 2017 For the first time ever, STP surpasses a key milestone: $100B in assets under administration (AUA) 2016 2016 Evidencing our rapid growth story, STP expands into new offices in West Chester, PA and Bangalore, India. 2016 STP acquires Financial Control Systems (FCS) to expand and strengthen services to plan sponsors across the country. 2014 STP India officially launches with the opening of an office in Bangalore, India. 2013 STP acquires new outsourcing clients from a Business Process Solutions provider. 2010 STP’s next generation, industry-leading software platform, SaaS Portal, launches. Platform offers revolutionary SaaS software, apps, interactive dashboards, and automated reporting. 2008 2008 The STP company launches with one foundation client, five team members, and $2B AUA, servicing funds, wealth, and institutional accounts.
